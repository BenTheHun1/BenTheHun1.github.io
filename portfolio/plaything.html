An spooky escape room, where you cooperate with a haunted doll to unlock memories of the past.

The Plaything is my Game Studio project, made on a team of 7 over the entirety of a semester, full-time.

I had the following roles:

  • Co-Producer, responsible for keeping everyone, and the project as a whole on track
  • Gameplay Programmer, responsible for programming the Player, Camera, Puzzles, and more
  • Game Designer, where I designed many of the game's systems and puzzles
