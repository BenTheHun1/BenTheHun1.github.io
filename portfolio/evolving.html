An incremental, idle game that "unfolds" by introducing new mini-games of other genres, that interlock and interact with each other (e.g. gameplay in a text-based RPG unlocks new food for your Tamagotchi-esque pet).

This was a solo project, so I was responsible for all aspects.

I did not complete as much of it as I wanted to. I do intend to return to this idea in the future. My ideal version would be a fusion of this and My Quarantine Life.
