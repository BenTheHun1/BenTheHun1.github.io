A child's distorted journey through his house, at night.

Imaginari was made as the final project for my Advanced Game Workshop class, on a team of 5, plus 2 Music & SFX members. I was the:

  • Producer, responsible for keeping everyone, and the project as a whole on track.
  • Programmer, primarily responsible for the Player Controller and Shop
  • Game Designer, where I created the original premise, and designed many of the game's systems.
