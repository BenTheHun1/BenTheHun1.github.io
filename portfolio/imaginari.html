A first-person platformer, where a child's imagination runs wild in the night, creating all kinds of imaginary obstacles on the way to retrieve his teddy bear.

This project was the final project for my Advanced Game Workshop class, on a team of 7. It had a troubled development, but we made it in the end.

I had the following roles:

  • Co-Producer, responsible for keeping everyone, and the project as a whole on track
  • Programmer, responsible for the Player Controller, moving platforms, and more
  • Game Designer, where I helped to created the original premise, and designed most of the game's systems
