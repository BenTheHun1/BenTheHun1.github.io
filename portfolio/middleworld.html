A text-based RPG, Middleworld: Chapter 1 set on a disc-shaped, world-between-worlds, which opens up to the other realities of the multiverse with the passing of time.

Middleworld: Chapter 1 was the final project for my Narrative Design class. This was a solo project, so I was responsible for all aspects.

A full-length, dramatically expanded and improved version of Middleworld is currently in development.
