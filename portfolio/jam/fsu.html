A chill visual novel, where you relax and go on a date with the beautiful Riberta.

This project was made during Fitchburg State University's Fall Game Jam 2022, on a team of 7, in 5 days. The theme was "Slow."

I had the following roles:

  • Producer, keeping everyone organized
  • Programmer, creating the Player Controller, and many of the mechanics
  • Scenario Writer, writing the entirety of the game's script and premise

I had the following roles:

To stop the evil Dr. Omega, Aqua and Magma journey throughout the land to acquire magic crystals. Aqua's levels are platformers, and Magma's are a top-down action game.

This project was made during Fitchburg State University's Fall Game Jam 2020, on a team of 4, in 5 days. The theme was "All Together Now."

I had the following roles:

  • Producer, keeping everyone organized
  • Programmer, for the Volcano/Lava levels exclusively
  • Designer, coming up with the original concept and mechanics
  • UI Designer, programming and design the HUD, and getting it functional across all levels/genres, as well as creating the main menu and level select

Who to trust? A mysterious voice on his radio leads Mr. Schitz to believe someone is out to get him in his town. He grasps a knife, to take matters into his own hands. What shall you do?

This project was made during Fitchburg State University's first ever Fall Game Jam in 2019, on a team of 4, in 4 days. The theme was "No One is the Enemy."

I had the following roles:

  • Producer, keeping everyone organized
  • Designer, coming up with the original concept and mechanics
  • 2D Artist, I made the tileset for and constructed the level in Godot, and designed the game's logo and ending art
  • Programmer, as I had previously developed the Untitled RPG (unreleased, but the source is on my GitHub) in Godot, which served as the basis for the project
  • Maintainer, as I independently created a post-jam version, fixing many of the game's bugs.
