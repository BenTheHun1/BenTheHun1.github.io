Mr. Schitz has Schizophrenia, and he imagines voices. Listen to them, or not.

Who is the Enemy? was made during Fitchburg State's First Annual Fall Game Jam, on a team of 4. I was the:

  • Producer, keeping everyone on track
  • Designer, coming up with the original concept and mechanics
  • 2D Artist, I made the tileset for and constructed the level in Godot, and designed the game's logo and ending art
  • Programmer, as I had previously developed the Untitled RPG (unreleased, but source available on GitHub) in Godot, which served as the basis for the project
  • Maintainer, as I independently created a post-jam version, fixing many of the game's bugs.
