In this Tamagotchi-like parody game, raise your little Kaiju to become strong and beautiful, and see how it evolves.

This project was made during GMTK Game Jam 2023, on a team of 4, in 3 days. The theme was "Roles Reversed."

I had the following roles:

  • Gameplay Programmer, responsible for programming the main systems and mechanics
  • 2D Artist, for all the pixel art.
