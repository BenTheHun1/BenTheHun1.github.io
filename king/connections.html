'Salem's Lot (1975)

  • Ben mentions the desire to continue driving past 'salem's Lot, into Chamberlain, the yet-to-be-destroyed setting of Carrie.

Night Shift (1978)

  • In Jerusalem's Lot, we learn that the Lot has always had issues with the occult. The nosferatu Charles encounters in Chapelwaite's basement is the first chronological reference to vampires.
  • In One For the Road, we learn the aftermath of the fire Ben and Mark set. Much of the town burns, not to be rebuilt. However, many vampires survived.
