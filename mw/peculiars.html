Peculiars

Select humans are blessed with Gifts of unknown origin

In 2028, the first Peculiars were discovered in Western England. After their exposure, they were found throughout the World. Non-Peculiars, or Ordinaries, had mixed emotions. Some thought it heralded the dawn of a new era for mankind. Others thought they were abominations that needed to be eradicated like a household pest.
