Armonia

A fantasy world of magic, on the terraformed Moon in the far future.

In the future, the Earth (known to Armonians as Terra), becomes more and more polluted. To prepare for an eventual evacuation, Scientists develop planet-wide terraforming technology, and fit a spacecraft with this tech, the Ark. Tensions between nations heighten, and global thermonuclear war commences, wiping out all life and leaving the planet uninhabitable. In a panic, the scientists board the spacecraft, having loaded it with various plants and animals. They successfully terraform the Moon, and they set up a primitive civilization there after crash landing. The Ark seems to have had an unintended side effect, however, influencing the rapid evolution of the animals they brought with them.
